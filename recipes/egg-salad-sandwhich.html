Egg Salad Sandwhich

Description

An egg salad sandwich is a classic lunchtime favorite. Egg salad sandwiches are known for their creamy and satisfying taste and are a popular choice for picnics, lunches, or a quick and easy meal at home.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Green onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
